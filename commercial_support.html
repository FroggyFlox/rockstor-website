An incident is defined as a question relating to a specific, discrete issue, and may involve several interactions with the Rockstor team prior to that issues's resolution. Here are some examples.

  1. Custom configuration or package install.

If your issue is caused by a Rockstor package software bug then there is no charge. In this case we prioritising the related bugfix.
